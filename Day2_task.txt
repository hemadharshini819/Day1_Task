
BLOCK 1 – APTITUDE (Profit & Loss)


1. A shopkeeper buys a book for ₹400 and sells it for ₹480.
Find the profit percentage.
ANS: 
     find the profit:
             = selling price - cost price
             = 480 - 400
             = 80

     Calculate the percentage:
             = (profit/cost price)*100
             = (80/400)*100
             = 20%

     The profit percentage is 20%

2. A man buys a watch for ₹1500 and sells it for ₹1200.
Find the loss percentage.
ANS: 
      Find the loss:
           = cost price - selling price
           = 1500 - 1200
           = 300

     calculate the percentage:
           =(loss/cost price)*100
           = (300/1500)*100
           = 20%

        The loss percentage is 20%

3. A trader marks a product 20% above the cost price and gives a 10% discount.
Find the final profit percentage.
ANS: 
      Let cost price = 100
     market price = 20% above the c.p
                  = 20 + 100
                  = 120

     Discount = gives a 10% discount in M.P
              = 10/100 * 120
              = 12

     selling price = market price - discount
                   = 120 - 12
                   = 108

     find the profit:
          profit = S.P - C.P
                 = 108 - 100
                 = 8%
 
        The final profit percentage is 8%


4. If the cost price of 5 pens is ₹100, what is the selling price of each pen to make 25% profit?
ANS:
       The cost of per pen:
              100/5 = 20 per pen ammount

       find the profit ammount:
             25% of 20 = 25/100 * 20
                       = 5

       find the Selling price
             selling price = profit + cost price
                           = 5 + 20
                           = 25

             The selling price is 25

5. A product is sold for ₹900 with a 10% loss.
Find the cost price.
ANS:
      cost price = (100/100 - loss)*selling price
                 = (100/100 - 10) * 900
                 = (100/90) * 900
                 = 1000

         The cost price is 1000
