APTITUDE (Time & Work)

1. A can complete a work in 10 days and B in 15 days.
How many days will they take together?
SOL:
      A = 10 Days and B = 15 days
      LCM of 10 and 15 = 30
      Total units =30

      A efficiency = 10/30 = 3
      B efficiency = 15/30 = 2
      combined efficiency = 3+2 = 5units
      together = 30/5 = 6days

2. A alone can do a work in 12 days.
After working for 4 days, B joins and work finishes in 4 more days.
Find B's efficiency.
SOL:
       Total Work = 12 units
       4 days alone: 4*1 = 4units
       Remaining work = 12-4 = 8units
       A and B finish 8 units and 4 more days
       Combined efficiency = 8/4 = 2units
       B's efficiency = 1unit

3. If 5 people can complete a work in 20 days,
how many people are needed to complete the work in 10 days?
SOL:
        5/20=100
        complete the work in 10 Days
          = 10/100
          = 10 peoples

4. A is twice as efficient as B.
If B takes 12 days to complete work, how many days will A take?
SOL:
       Efficiency Ratio (A:B) = 2:1
       Time Ratio (A:B) = 1:2
       If B = 12 days, then 1 part = 6 days.
       A will take = 6days
