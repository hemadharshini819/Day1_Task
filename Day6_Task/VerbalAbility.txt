VERBAL ABILITY (Advanced)

Reading Passage:

Databases are at the heart of modern applications. Efficient storage,
retrieval, and management of data are essential for building scalable
software products. Companies rely on well-designed databases to ensure
data consistency and performance.

Questions:

1. Why are databases important in modern applications?
ANS: Databases are important because they help in efficient storage,
retrieval, and management of data.
2. What does data consistency mean?
ANS: Data consistency means that the data remains accurate, reliable,
and the same across the database without errors or contradictions.

3. Why do companies need well-designed databases?
ANS: Companies need well-designed databases to ensure data consistency
and improve performance of their applications.
