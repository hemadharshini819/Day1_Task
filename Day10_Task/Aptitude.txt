APTITUDE (Speed, Time & Distance)

1. A car travels 60 km in 1 hour. What is the speed?
SOL:
     Distance = 60KM
     Time     = 1 hour
      Speed = Distance / Time
            = 60 / 1
      Speed = 60

2. A train travels at 50 km/hr. How long will it take to travel 150 km?
SOL:
        Train travel = 50km/hr
     Total travel    = 150km
      Time = Distance / Speed
           = 150 / 50
      Time = 3 hours

3. If a person walks at 5 km/hr, how far will they walk in 3 hours?
SOL:
     person walks = 5km/hr
         Distance = Speed / Time
                  = 5 * 3
        Distance  = 15 hours 
    
4. Two trains move in opposite direction at 40 km/hr and 60 km/hr.
Find relative speed.
SOL:
      Trian1 = 40km/hr
      Train2 = 60km/hr
      relative Speed = 40 + 60
                     = 100 km/hr

5. A car travels first half distance at 40 km/hr and second half at 60 km/hr.
Find average speed.
SOL:
        1st dis = 40km/hr
        2nd dis = 60km/hr
        Average speed Formula = x + y/ 2xy
                              = 40 + 60/ 2*40*60
                              = 100/4800
             Average speed    = 48km/hr
